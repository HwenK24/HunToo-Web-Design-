Haunted Spot

First World Hotel, Genting Highlands, Pahang 💀💀💀💀💀

This resort hotel is said to be haunted by the ghosts of suicide victims who lost everything at the casino. Children cry and refuse to go near parts of the hotel. Healthy guests fall ill for no apparent reason. You can smell incense, which the Chinese believe is food for ghosts. Some of the 6118 rooms are said to be cursed and the hotel never rents them out to guests, even when the hotel is at full occupancy. The entire 21st floor is said to be cursed, and the elevator always skips that floor, for some reason.
