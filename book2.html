Over 15 years in the making, this is a mid-career retrospective by Malaysias prince of darkness Tunku Halim.

Featuring twenty of his best short stories such as Biggest Baddest Bomoh, Mr Petronas, The Rape of Martha Teoh, Night of the Pontianak, Malay Magick and Gravediggers Kiss, this is a collection that will make you tingle in all sorts of places.
