About Us

We go around Malaysia's haunted spots to ghost hunt and to find out the truth behind these happenings. We make videos to share our experiences to you, give scientific and logical explanation on supernatural occurrence and also share our thoughts on paranormal horror films, books and games so you can easily know more about supernatural things. Enjoy your stay here, if you are spooked, it's our pleasure. :)

P.s. Do remember to be respectful and set boundaries when it comes to ghost hunting to not invade and offend the supernatural beings.
